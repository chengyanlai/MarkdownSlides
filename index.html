Model of XAS

Effective Hamiltonian

$\mathcal{H}_{\text{FHM}}=-t\sum_{\langle ij \rangle,\sigma}(d^\dagger_{i\sigma}d_{j\sigma}+h.c.)+U_0\sum_in_{i\uparrow}n_{i\downarrow}$

$\mathcal{H}(t)=\mathcal{H}_{\text{FHM}}+\mathcal{H}_{\text{ch}}$, where $\mathcal{H}_{\text{ch}}=V_{\text{ch}}n_p + U_{\text{ch}} n_{p\uparrow}n_{p\downarrow}$.

XAS spectrum

\begin{eqnarray} I_{\text{XAS}}(\omega)&\propto&\sum_\sigma\sum_f \vert\langle f \vert d_{i\sigma}^\dagger \vert 0 \rangle\vert^2 \delta(E_f-E_0-\hbar\omega)\\ &=& \frac{1}{\pi}\sum_\sigma\text{Im}\left[ -i\int_0^\infty dt e^{i(\hbar\omega+E_0)t}e^{-\Gamma t}\color{green}{\langle 0 \vert d_{p\sigma} }\color{blue}{e^{-i\mathcal{H}t} d_{p\sigma}^\dagger \vert 0 \rangle} \right]. \end{eqnarray}
