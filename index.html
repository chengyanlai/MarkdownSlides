Photoinduced order: Ultrafast pump pulse driven systems

Pump pulse Hamiltonian
$\mathcal{H}_0\rightarrow\mathcal{H}_{\text{pump}}=-J\sum_{\langle ij \rangle,\sigma}$($e^{iA(t)}$$c^\dagger_{i\sigma}c_{j\sigma}+h.c.)$
Gaussian pulse
$A(t)=A_0e^{-(t-t_d)^2/2\tau^2}\cos[\Omega(t-t_d)+\phi]$

$\tau$: Width of the pulse (number of pump cycles)

$t_d$: Time delay for vanished pulse at $t=0$

Different frequency and strength of influence.

$\vert\Psi(t)\rangle\!=\!e^{-i(\mathcal{H}_{pump}+\mathcal{H}_{int})t}\vert\Psi(t\rightarrow-\infty)\rangle$

SDW side ($U=9J$, $V=3J$)

  • Both SDW and BOW orders are suppressed.
  • The charge order gets enhanced.
  • Stronger response near $\Omega\!=\!6J$ (optical gap).

Within BOW regime ($U=6J$, $V=3J$)

  • Both SDW and BOW orders are suppressed.
  • The charge order gets strongly enhanced.
  • Stronger response near $\Omega\!=\!J$.

CDW side ($U=3J$, $V=3J$)

  • Both CDW order is suppressed.
  • The BOW gets enhanced.
  • Stronger response near $\Omega\!=\!6J$.

(Non-) Equilibrium spectral function

$A(p,q,t_f,t_i)\!=\!\langle\Psi(t_i)\vert\{c^\dagger_p(t_f),c_q(t_i)\}\vert\Psi(t_i)\rangle$

$A(k,\omega)\!=\!N_s^{-1}\sum_{p,q=1}^{N_s}e^{ik(p-q)}\int_{t_i}^{t_f}dte^{i\omega t-\Gamma t}A(p,q,t_f,t_i)$

Conclusion and outlook

  • Photo-induced charge order in extended Hubbard model at large $U$.
  • Possible photo-enhanced bond order in extended Hubbard model near CDW edge as the pump pulse drives the state into part of excited states.
  • (Out-of-) Equilibrium angle-resolved spectroscopy in 1D strongly correlated systems can be studied by MPS in time domain.
  • Stay tuned: Observation from Time-Resolved, Angle-Resolved PhotoEmission Spectroscopy?
