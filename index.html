Conclusions

  • Memory effects are shown to exist in noninteracting quantum systems when a flat band emerges.
  • Zero temperature non-interacting fermionic system and bosonic system at intermediate temperature are better candidates, but not BEC.
  • Applications on atomtronic device - accelerometer and memory device.
  • QSSC exist in both fermionic and bosonic gases, except non-interacting boson.
  • Quantum memory effects for both initial states and switching schemes can be observed in both statists.
