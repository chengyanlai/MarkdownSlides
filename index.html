Conclusions

  • Relaxation: Phys. Rev. A 93, 053617 (2016)
    • Magnetization/Persistant current lags due to relaxation.
  • Flat Band: Phys. Rev. Applied 5, 034001 (2016)
    • Memory of density distribution are shown to exist in noninteracting quantum systems when a flat band emerges.
    • Zero temperature non-interacting fermionic system and bosonic system at intermediate temperature are better candidates, but not BEC.
  • Interaction: Phys. Rev. A 96, 033628 (2017)
    • Steady-state currents exist in both fermionic and bosonic gases, except non-interacting boson.
    • Quantum memory effects for both initial states and switching schemes can be observed.
