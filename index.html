Quantum Impurity Models of Kondo Physics

Single impurity interact with itinerant free fermions and bosonic bath.

  • Fermion bath and impurity: Kondo coherence.
  • Bosonic bath and impurity: Spin fluctuation.

Consequences: Absence or presence of Kondo coherence or Critical behavior.

Model and Bath Discretization

Bose-Fermi-Anderson impurity model

$H_{f}=-J\sum_\sigma\sum_{i=1}^{L-1}(c^\dagger_{i\sigma}c_{i+1\sigma}+h.c.)-V_f\sum_\sigma(c^\dagger_{0\sigma}c_{1\sigma}+h.c.)+U_f(n_{0\uparrow}-\frac12)(n_{0\downarrow}-\frac12)$

$H_{b}=\sum_q\omega_q\phi^\dagger_{q}\phi_{q}$

$H_{int}=\tilde{g}S_z\sum_q(\phi^\dagger_{-q}+\phi_{q})$, where $S_z=\frac12(n_{0\uparrow}-n_{0\downarrow})$.

Bosonic Bath:

$B(\omega)=\begin{cases} B_0\omega_0(\frac{\omega}{\omega_0})^s\quad 0<\omega<\omega_0, \\ 0\quad\text{otherwise}. \end{cases}$

Discretization
  • Logarithmic space of $\omega$.
  • Standard Lanczos technique to 1D chain.

Signatures in Equilibrium

$L_b\!=\!18$ and $L_f\!=\!54$

Kondo energy $\propto V^2_f/U_f$.

Larger $B_0$ $\Rightarrow$ stronger coupling to bosonic degrees of freedom.

$X_{-1} = \langle b_{-1} + b^\dagger_{-1} \rangle$: oscillation of the bosonic mode next to the impurity.

Local Density of States

$L_b\!=\!14$, $L_f\!=\!42$, $U\!=\!8J$, $V_f\!=\!J$

  • $\rho(\omega=0)=0.5$
  • FWHM gives an estimation of Kondo energy scale

Pump Pulse Effect

Time-dependent Peierls phase on hopping of electrons

$H_{f}(t)=-J\color{red}{e^{iA(t)}}\sum_\sigma\sum_{i=1}^{L-1}(c^\dagger_{i\sigma}c_{i+1\sigma}+h.c.)-V_f\color{red}{e^{iA(t)}}\sum_\sigma(c^\dagger_{0\sigma}c_{1\sigma}+h.c.)\\\qquad\quad+U_f(n_{0\uparrow}-\frac12)(n_{0\downarrow}-\frac12)$
$H_b$ and $H_{int}$ unchanged.
The Pieles phase is a semiclassical form of the pulse field:
$A(t)=A_0e^{-t^2/2\tau^2}\cos\Omega t,$

Fidelity of the wave function

$\Omega\!=\!4J$ and $\tau\!=\!0.5$
